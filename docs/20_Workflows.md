# Workflows

## 1. Buyer On-Site With A New Unit

### Goal
Quickly understand what’s inside and estimate value.

### Steps

1. Open unit & take wide-angle and detail photos
2. Upload photos to SimLay
3. SimLay creates draft inventory
4. User reviews & edits items
5. SimLay provides:
   - item list
   - category summary
   - rough value range
6. Export report or share with partner

### Output
Fast decision support — buy, pass, or negotiate.

---

## 2. Remote Review With Partner

### Goal
Collaborate on evaluation.

### Steps

1. Create a project
2. Upload images
3. Invite collaborator
4. Discuss inside app (comments/notes)
5. Finalize valuation

---

## 3. Resale Preparation

### Goal
Track inventory across selling channels.

### Steps

1. Tag items as “listed”, “sold”, “donated”, etc.
2. Track price & profit
3. Generate summary reports

---

## 4. Documentation / Insurance

### Goal
Maintain records of inventory.

### Steps

1. Upload photos
2. Create inventory record
3. Export PDF/CSV
4. Archive project
